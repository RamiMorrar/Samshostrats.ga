Hello and welcome to SamSho Strats, a website aimed to give new players all the essential information they need about the game. Information about fighting games nowadays can be very decentralized and hard to find, which makes it difficult for new players to find what they're looking for. This website aims to change that in order to lead new players on the right path. This website aims to be the encyclopedia for the latest SamSho title, with pieces of information curated from all over the internet like video tutorials unique to each character, frame data, notable players for each character, and match videos!
For any suggestions, or if you want to contribute, please feel free to hit the Contact page on the top.
